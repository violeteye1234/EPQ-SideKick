"Development of practical skills resulting in a performance, eg performing music, drama, sport"-Pearson Edexcel

Artefact

"E.g. making a painting or sculpture, designing a piece of furniture or a garment, creating a website, solving an engineering/construction problem, producing a piece of graphic design." -Pearson Edexcel
